पुष्पी पादपों में लैंगिक जनन
Flower | Pollination | Fertilization | Seed
▼
पुष्पी पादपों में जनन पुष्प के माध्यम से होता है। पुष्प एक रूपांतरित प्ररोह है जिसमें नर (पुंकेसर) और मादा (स्त्रीकेसर) जनन अंग होते हैं। परागण, निषेचन और बीज निर्माण क्रमिक प्रक्रियाएं हैं।
🌸 पुष्प की संरचना | Flower Structure
  • बाह्यदलपुंज (Calyx): हरे बाह्यदल — पुष्प की रक्षा करते हैं
  • दलपुंज (Corolla): रंगीन पंखुड़ियां — परागणकर्ताओं को आकर्षित करती हैं
  • पुंकेसर (Stamen): नर जनन अंग — परागकोष + तंतु। परागकोष में सूक्ष्मबीजाणु बनते हैं
  • स्त्रीकेसर (Pistil): मादा जनन अंग — वर्तिकाग्र + वर्तिका + अंडाशय
  • अंडाशय (Ovary): अंडप से बना; बीजांड (Ovule) होते हैं जिनमें भ्रूणकोष होता है
🌼 सूक्ष्मबीजाणुजनन | Microsporogenesis
  • परागकोष: Anther में 4 microsporangia होते हैं
  • Microspore mother cells → Meiosis → 4 Microspores (Tetrad)
  • परागकण (Pollen grain): Exine (sporopollenin) + Intine। नर युग्मकोद्भिद होता है
  • Pollen में 2 cells — Vegetative cell + Generative cell
  • Pollen viability: Wheat/rice में 30 min; members of Poaceae में अधिक
🌺 गुरुबीजाणुजनन | Megasporogenesis
  • Nucellus में Megaspore mother cell → Meiosis → 4 Megaspores (3 degenerate, 1 functional)
  • भ्रूणकोष (Embryo sac): 8 nucleated, 7 celled structure
  • Egg cell (1) + Synergids (2) + Antipodal cells (3) + Central cell (2 polar nuclei)
  • Polygonum type — most common type of embryo sac
🦋 परागण | Pollination
  • Autogamy: Same flower — Cleistogamy (closed flower, e.g., Viola, Commelina)
  • Geitonogamy: Different flowers of same plant — genetically same as autogamy
  • Xenogamy: Different plant — true cross pollination, genetic variation
  • Wind pollination (Anemophily): Light pollen, large stigma, e.g., Grass, Wheat
  • Insect pollination (Entomophily): Coloured/scented flowers, sticky pollen
  • Water pollination (Hydrophily): Vallisneria, Hydrilla — rare
  • Outbreeding devices: Dichogamy, Self-incompatibility, Heterostyly
💥 निषेचन | Fertilization
  • Pollen tube grows through style → enters ovule → Synergid mein filiform apparatus
  • Double Fertilization (S.G. Nawaschin, 1898): Angiosperms की विशेषता
  • Sperm 1 + Egg → Zygote (2n) → Embryo
  • Sperm 2 + Polar nuclei → Primary endosperm nucleus (3n) → Endosperm
  • Syngamy: Egg + sperm fusion | Triple fusion: 2 polar nuclei + sperm
🌱 निषेचन के बाद | Post-fertilization
  • Zygote → Embryo (Embryogenesis) | Ovule → Seed | Ovary → Fruit
  • Endosperm types: Nuclear, Cellular, Helobial
  • Coconut milk = Nuclear endosperm (liquid); Coconut meat = Cellular endosperm
  • Parthenocarpy: Seedless fruit without fertilization (banana, grapes)
  • Apomixis: Seed without fertilization (Citrus, Mango) — genetic uniformity
  • Polyembryony: More than one embryo in seed (Citrus, Groundnut)
⭐ PNST Important Points
  • Double fertilization — Angiosperms की विशेषता
  • Sporopollenin — hardest natural substance, pollen wall में
  • Embryo sac = 7 celled, 8 nucleate
  • Cleistogamy — self pollination, closed flowers
  • Coconut milk = liquid nuclear endosperm
  • Apomixis = no fertilization, seeds form

02
मानव जनन
Gametogenesis | Menstrual Cycle | Fertilization | Embryo
▼
मनुष्य लैंगिक रूप से जनन करते हैं। नर में शुक्राणु और मादा में अंडाणु बनते हैं। निषेचन, आरोपण, भ्रूण विकास और प्रसव क्रमिक प्रक्रियाएं हैं।
♂ नर जनन तंत्र | Male Reproductive System
  • Testes: Scrotum में स्थित (2-2.5°C कम temperature)। Seminiferous tubules में spermatogenesis
  • Leydig cells: Testosterone produce करती हैं (interstitial cells)
  • Sertoli cells: Sperm को nutrition प्रदान करती हैं ("Nurse cells")
  • Duct system: Rete testis → Vasa efferentia → Epididymis → Vas deferens → Urethra
  • Accessory glands: Seminal vesicle (60% semen) + Prostate + Cowper's gland (bulbourethral)
  • Semen में fructose (sperm ko energy) + enzymes + mucus होते हैं
♀ मादा जनन तंत्र | Female Reproductive System
  • Ovaries: Bilateral — Graafian follicle में oocyte develop होता है
  • Fallopian tube (Oviduct): Infundibulum (fimbriae) → Ampulla → Isthmus। Fertilization यहीं होती है
  • Uterus (Womb): Perimetrium + Myometrium + Endometrium। Implantation यहां होता है
  • Vagina: Birth canal — copulation और delivery दोनों के लिए
🔬 युग्मकजनन | Gametogenesis
  • Spermatogenesis: Spermatogonia → Primary spermatocyte → Secondary spermatocyte → Spermatids → Spermatozoa
  • Spermiogenesis: Spermatid → Sperm (mature) transformation
  • Sperm: Head (acrosome + nucleus) + Middle piece (mitochondria) + Tail (flagellum)
  • Oogenesis: Birth से पहले ही primary oocyte बन जाते हैं; puberty पर complete होते हैं
  • Ovulation — Graafian follicle से secondary oocyte निकलता है (LH surge से)
📅 ऋतुस्राव चक्र | Menstrual Cycle
  • 28 दिन का cycle — Menarche (puberty) से Menopause (45-50 yr) तक
  • Menstrual phase (1-5): Endometrium shed होता है — bleeding
  • Follicular/Proliferative (6-13): FSH → follicle develop, Estrogen बढ़ता है
  • Ovulation (Day 14): LH surge → Graafian follicle rupture → Ovum release
  • Luteal phase (15-28): Corpus luteum → Progesterone (pregnancy maintain)
  • Pregnancy में menstrual cycle रुक जाता है
🤰 निषेचन से प्रसव | Fertilization to Parturition
  • Fertilization: Fallopian tube के Ampulla में — sperm + secondary oocyte
  • Zygote → Cleavage → Morula → Blastocyst → Implantation (Uterus, 7th day)
  • Placenta: Embryo-mother के बीच exchange organ। HCG, Progesterone, Estrogen secrete करता है
  • Gastrulation: 3 germ layers — Ectoderm, Mesoderm, Endoderm
  • Gestation period = 9 months (38-40 weeks)
  • Parturition: Oxytocin द्वारा uterine contractions → Baby delivery
  • Colostrum: पहला पीला दूध — IgA antibodies से भरपूर। बच्चे की immunity के लिए जरूरी
⭐ PNST Important Points
  • Leydig cells → Testosterone | Sertoli cells → Sperm nutrition
  • Fertilization — Ampulla of Fallopian tube में
  • Placenta → HCG (pregnancy test में), Progesterone, Estrogen
  • Colostrum → IgA antibodies (passive immunity)
  • Cowper's gland = Bulbourethral gland — Lubrication
  • LH surge → Ovulation (Day 14)

03
जनन स्वास्थ्य
STD | Birth Control | MTP | Infertility
▼
WHO के अनुसार जनन स्वास्थ्य = शारीरिक, भावनात्मक, व्यवहार संबंधी और सामाजिक — सभी पहलुओं में स्वस्थ। भारत सरकार ने 1951 में Family Planning Programme शुरू किया।
📊 जनसंख्या विस्फोट | Population Explosion
  • India — 1900: 25 cr → 1947: 35 cr → 2000: 100 cr → 2011: 121 cr
  • कारण: Death rate में कमी, Birth rate अधिक, Medical facilities बेहतर
  • RCH (Reproductive and Child Health Care): India का national programme
🚫 जन्म नियंत्रण | Birth Control Methods
  • Natural methods: Periodic abstinence (Safe period: Day 10-17), Coitus interruptus, Lactational amenorrhea (6 months)
  • Barriers: Condom (male/female) — STD से भी बचाता है। Diaphragm, Cervical cap, Vault
  • IUDs: Non-medicated (Lippes loop), Copper releasing (CuT, Cu7 — 3-5 yr), Hormone releasing (Progestasert, LNG-20)
  • Oral pills (Saheli): Estrogen-progesterone combination। Ovulation रोकती हैं। "Saheli" — non-steroidal, India में developed
  • Emergency contraception: Unprotected sex के 72 hr के भीतर
  • Surgical methods: Vasectomy (male — vas deferens cut) | Tubectomy (female — fallopian tube cut)
⚕️ MTP (Medical Termination of Pregnancy)
  • India में 1971 से legal (MTP Act)
  • First trimester (0-12 weeks) — relatively safe
  • Second trimester (13-24 weeks) — only special cases
  • Sex-selective MTP illegal है
🦠 यौन संचारित रोग | STDs
  • Gonorrhea (Neisseria gonorrhoeae — bacteria)
  • Syphilis (Treponema pallidum — bacteria)
  • Genital herpes, Genital warts (virus)
  • AIDS: HIV (Retrovirus) — T-lymphocytes destroy करता है। Blood/sexual contact/mother to child से
  • Chlamydiasis, Trichomoniasis (most common STD)
👶 बांझपन | Infertility & ART
  • IVF (In-vitro fertilization): Test tube baby — fertilization lab में, Embryo transfer uterus में
  • ZIFT: Zygote Intra Fallopian Transfer
  • GIFT: Gamete Intra Fallopian Transfer
  • ICSI: Intracytoplasmic Sperm Injection
  • AI (Artificial Insemination): Donor sperm — uterus/cervix में
⭐ PNST Important Points
  • Saheli — non-steroidal oral contraceptive pill (India)
  • Copper-T → Phagocytosis of sperm, Cervical mucus thick
  • Vasectomy = male, Tubectomy = female sterilization
  • First test tube baby = Louise Brown (1978, UK)
  • Lactational amenorrhea — 6 months effective

वंशागति के सिद्धांत एवं विभिन्नता
Mendel | Monohybrid | Dihybrid | Sex Determination | Disorders
▼
Gregor Johann Mendel (Father of Genetics) ने 1866 में Pisum sativum (Garden pea) पर प्रयोग करके वंशागति के नियम प्रस्तुत किए।
🌱 Mendel के प्रयोग | Mendel's Experiments
  • 7 characteristics — Seed shape, Seed color, Pod shape, Pod color, Flower color, Flower position, Plant height
  • Monohybrid cross: F1 = Tall (all), F2 = 3 Tall : 1 Dwarf (3:1 phenotype ratio)
  • Dihybrid cross: F2 = 9:3:3:1 ratio
  • Test cross: Unknown genotype × Homozygous recessive (tt)
📜 Mendel के नियम | Mendel's Laws
  • Law of Dominance: एक character के दो contrasting forms में से एक dominant होता है
  • Law of Segregation (Purity of gametes): Alleles gamete formation पर अलग हो जाते हैं। Universally applicable
  • Law of Independent Assortment: Two genes independently separate होते हैं। Linked genes में लागू नहीं होता
🧬 वंशागति के अन्य प्रकार | Other Inheritance Patterns
  • Incomplete dominance: F1 intermediate (e.g., Red × White = Pink Snapdragon). F2 = 1:2:1
  • Codominance: Both alleles express equally (e.g., ABO blood group — IA, IB codominant; i recessive)
  • Polygenic inheritance: Multiple genes → single trait (e.g., Skin color, Height)
  • Pleiotropy: Single gene → multiple effects (e.g., Phenylketonuria)
  • Multiple alleles: ABO blood group — IA, IB, i (3 alleles, but 2 per individual)
🧑‍🔬 लिंग निर्धारण | Sex Determination
  • Human: Female = XX, Male = XY। Male determines sex of child
  • Drosophila: XX = Female, XY = Male (same as human)
  • Grasshopper: XX = Female, XO = Male (no Y chromosome)
  • Birds, Butterflies: ZW = Female, ZZ = Male
  • Honeybee: Fertilized = Female (diploid), Unfertilized = Male drone (haploid) — Haplodiploidy
🔗 लिंग संबद्ध वंशागति | Sex-linked Inheritance
  • Haemophilia: X-linked recessive। ज्यादातर males affected। Royal disease
  • Colour blindness: X-linked recessive। Males more affected
  • Carrier female = XHXh → Pass to sons (50% chance)
🏥 आनुवांशिक विकार | Genetic Disorders
  • Down's syndrome (Trisomy 21): 47 chromosomes। Extra chromosome 21। Mental retardation, characteristic face
  • Klinefelter's (47, XXY): Male — Gynaecomastia, sterile
  • Turner's (45, XO): Female — Sterile, underdeveloped breasts
  • Phenylketonuria: Autosomal recessive — Phenylalanine metabolism defect, Mental retardation
  • Sickle cell anaemia: Autosomal recessive — HbS (Valine instead of Glutamic acid in β-globin)
  • Thalassemia: Autosomal recessive — αβ globin defect, RBC fragile
⭐ PNST Important Points
  • Mendel = Father of Genetics | Garden pea (7 characters)
  • Monohybrid F2 = 3:1 | Dihybrid F2 = 9:3:3:1
  • ABO blood group = Codominance + Multiple alleles
  • Haemophilia = X-linked recessive = Royal disease
  • Down's syndrome = Trisomy 21 (47 chromosomes)
  • Sickle cell — Valine replaces Glutamic acid

वंशागति का आणविक आधार
DNA | Replication | Transcription | Translation | Genetic Code
▼
DNA (Deoxyribonucleic acid) अधिकांश जीवों में आनुवांशिक पदार्थ है। Watson और Crick ने 1953 में Double Helix Model प्रस्तुत किया।
🧬 DNA संरचना | DNA Structure
  • Nucleotide = Sugar (Deoxyribose) + Phosphate + Nitrogenous base
  • Purines: Adenine (A), Guanine (G) | Pyrimidines: Cytosine (C), Thymine (T)
  • Chargaff's rule: A=T, G=C (A+G = T+C, Purines = Pyrimidines)
  • Two strands = Antiparallel (5'→3' and 3'→5')
  • H-bonds: A-T = 2 H-bonds, G-C = 3 H-bonds
  • Pitch = 3.4 nm, 10 base pairs per turn, Width = 2 nm
  • RNA: Ribose sugar, Uracil (instead of Thymine), Single stranded
🔬 Genetic Material की खोज | Discovery
  • Griffith (1928): Transformation experiment — S-strain (virulent) + Heat-killed = R-strain virulent हो गई
  • Avery, MacLeod, McCarty (1944): DNA = Transforming principle prove किया
  • Hershey & Chase (1952): Bacteriophage experiment → DNA = Genetic material
  • Tobacco Mosaic Virus (TMV), QB phage — RNA = Genetic material (exception)
🔁 DNA Replication
  • Semi-conservative: Meselson & Stahl (1958) ने E.coli में prove किया। N15-N14 experiment
  • Replication fork: Helicase (H-bonds तोड़ता है) → SSB proteins (stabilize) → Primase (RNA primer)
  • DNA Polymerase III: Leading strand (continuous, 5'→3') + Lagging strand (discontinuous, Okazaki fragments)
  • DNA Polymerase I: Primer remove करता है → DNA ligase → Okazaki fragments join
📝 Transcription (DNA → RNA)
  • Template strand (Antisense) से mRNA बनती है
  • Prokaryotes: Translation साथ-साथ होती है (no nuclear membrane)
  • Eukaryotes: hnRNA → Splicing → mRNA (Exons join, Introns remove)
  • Types of RNA: mRNA (message), tRNA (adapter/clover leaf), rRNA (structural)
🔤 आनुवांशिक कूट | Genetic Code
  • Triplet codon — 3 bases = 1 amino acid
  • 64 codons total — 61 sense codons + 3 stop codons (UAA, UAG, UGA)
  • AUG: Start codon (Methionine) | Degenerate: One AA = multiple codons
  • Universal: Same codons in all organisms (almost)
  • Wobble hypothesis: Crick — third codon position flexible
⚙️ Translation (mRNA → Protein)
  • Ribosome (rRNA + protein): 70S (Prokaryote) = 50S + 30S; 80S (Eukaryote) = 60S + 40S
  • tRNA = Adapter molecule, Anticodon loop
  • Steps: Activation → Initiation → Elongation → Termination
  • Aminoacyl tRNA synthetase: AA + tRNA attach करता है

विकास
Origin of Life | Darwin | Hardy-Weinberg | Evidences
▼
Evolution = समय के साथ जीवों में होने वाले परिवर्तन। पृथ्वी पर जीवन की उत्पत्ति लगभग 4 अरब वर्ष पहले हुई।
🌍 जीवन की उत्पत्ति | Origin of Life
  • Oparin (Russia) & Haldane (England) — Abiogenic theory (Chemical evolution)
  • Miller-Urey Experiment (1953): CH4, NH3, H2O, H2 → Electric discharge → Amino acids
  • First life forms = Chemoheterotrophs, Anaerobic, 2000 million years ago
  • Cyanobacteria → Oxygen → Ozone layer formation → Aerobic organisms
📚 विकास के प्रमाण | Evidences for Evolution
  • Homologous organs: Same structure, different function. Common ancestry. (Forelimb of whale, bat, cheetah, human)
  • Analogous organs: Different structure, same function. Convergent evolution. (Wings of butterfly & bird)
  • Vestigial organs: Degenerated organs (Appendix, ear muscles, body hair in humans)
  • Fossil record: Past organisms preserved. Connecting links (Archaeopteryx = reptile + bird)
  • Biochemical evidence: Cytochrome-c protein — same in all organisms
🧬 Darwin की Theory | Darwin's Theory
  • Natural Selection: Survival of the fittest — Herbert Spencer
  • HMS Beagle voyage → Galapagos islands → Finches (adaptive radiation)
  • Overproduction + Struggle + Variation + Survival → Evolution
  • Lamarck: Use & Disuse + Inheritance of acquired characters (Wrong!)
  • Hugo de Vries: Mutation theory (Saltation = Macro evolution in single step)
⚖️ Hardy-Weinberg Principle
  • Allele frequencies in population remain constant generation to generation (if no evolution)
  • p² + 2pq + q² = 1 (p + q = 1)
  • Disturbing factors: Gene mutation, Gene migration (Gene flow), Genetic drift (Founder effect, Bottle-neck), Natural selection, Non-random mating
  • Genetic drift: Random changes in allele frequency in small populations
🦎 मानव विकास | Human Evolution
  • Dryopithecus (Ape-like) → Ramapithecus → Australopithecus (4-5 mya, Africa)
  • Homo habilis (2 mya) → Homo erectus (1.5 mya, fire) → Homo sapiens neanderthalensis → Homo sapiens sapiens
  • Cro-magnon man = Modern man precursor (cave paintings)
⭐ PNST Important Points
  • Miller-Urey experiment = Amino acids from inorganic molecules
  • Homologous = Same structure, Diff function (Common ancestor)
  • Analogous = Diff structure, Same function (Convergent evolution)
  • Archaeopteryx = Connecting link (Reptile + Bird)
  • Hardy-Weinberg = p²+2pq+q²=1
  • Adaptive radiation = Darwin's finches, Marsupials

Pathogens | Immunity | AIDS | Cancer | Drugs
▼
WHO के अनुसार स्वास्थ्य = शरीर, मन और समाज की पूर्ण भलाई। रोग = किसी भी कारण से normal functioning में disturbance।
🦠 रोगजनक | Common Pathogens
  • Typhoid: Salmonella typhi (bacteria) — contaminated water/food। Widal test
  • Pneumonia: Streptococcus pneumoniae / Haemophilus influenzae। Alveoli filled with fluid
  • Common cold: Rhino virus। No vaccine
  • Malaria: Plasmodium (P. falciparum most dangerous) — Female Anopheles mosquito vector
  • Amoebiasis: Entamoeba histolytica — contaminated water, "amoebic dysentery"
  • Ascariasis: Ascaris lumbricoides (roundworm) — intestinal infection
  • Elephantiasis (Filariasis): Wuchereria bancrofti — lymph vessels block। Female Culex mosquito
  • Ringworm: Microsporum, Trichophyton (fungi) — dry scaly lesions
🛡️ प्रतिरक्षा | Immunity
  • Innate immunity: Non-specific, present from birth। Barriers: Physical, Physiological, Cellular, Cytokine
  • Acquired immunity: Antigen-specific, memory (Anamnestic response)
  • Active immunity: Own antibodies bante hain। Slow, long-lasting. Vaccination
  • Passive immunity: Ready-made antibodies दिए जाते हैं। Fast, short-lived। Mother→fetus (IgG), Colostrum (IgA)
  • B-lymphocytes: Humoral immunity (antibody-mediated) — IgG, IgA, IgM, IgE, IgD
  • T-lymphocytes: Cell-mediated immunity — Cytotoxic T cells, Helper T cells
  • Interferon: Cytokines — virus-infected cells release → neighbouring cells protect
🔴 AIDS | HIV
  • HIV: Human Immunodeficiency Virus — Retrovirus (RNA genome)
  • Spread: Sexual contact, Blood transfusion, Infected needles, Mother to child
  • HIV → Helper T-cells (CD4+) destroy → Immune system collapse → Opportunistic infections
  • ELISA test: Detection | ART (Anti-retroviral therapy): Treatment (not cure)
🦠 Cancer
  • Uncontrolled cell division — Proto-oncogene → Oncogene (mutation)
  • Carcinogens: Tobacco, Radiation, Chemicals, Viruses (Oncogenic viruses)
  • Types: Carcinoma (epithelial), Sarcoma (connective), Leukemia (blood), Lymphoma
  • Metastasis: Cancer cells spread to other sites via blood/lymph
  • Treatment: Surgery, Radiotherapy, Chemotherapy, Immunotherapy
💊 नशीले पदार्थ | Drugs & Alcohol
  • Opioids: Morphine, Heroin (Diacetylmorphine) — Papaver somniferum
  • Cannabinoids: THC — Cannabis sativa (hemp plant)
  • Cocaine: Erythroxylum coca — Dopamine release
  • Alcohol → CNS depression, liver cirrhosis
⭐ PNST Important Points
  • Malaria — Plasmodium falciparum (most dangerous), Female Anopheles
  • Elephantiasis — Wuchereria bancrofti, Female Culex
  • HIV = Retrovirus, attacks CD4+ T-lymphocytes
  • Active immunity = long lasting | Passive = short lived
  • Heroin (Smack) = Diacetylmorphine (Papaver somniferum)
  • ELISA test — AIDS detection

जैव प्रौद्योगिकी: सिद्धांत व प्रक्रम
Restriction Enzymes | PCR | Recombinant DNA | Vectors
▼
Biotechnology = Biology + Technology। EFB (European Federation of Biotechnology) — "New biotechnology": Recombinant DNA technology। Stanley Cohen & Herbert Boyer (1972) = First recombinant DNA।
✂️ Restriction Enzymes
  • "Molecular scissors" — Specific DNA sequences पर cut करते हैं
  • EcoRI: 5'-G↓AATTC-3' (First restriction enzyme isolated)
  • Palindromic sequences: 5'→3' पर same as 3'→5' on complementary strand
  • Sticky ends (staggered cuts) — DNA fragments को join करने में आसान
  • DNA Ligase = "Molecular glue" — DNA fragments को join करता है
🔬 Recombinant DNA Technology
  • rDNA: Alien DNA + Vector DNA
  • Vectors: Plasmid (pBR322), Bacteriophage, Cosmid, BAC, YAC
  • pBR322: 2 antibiotic resistance genes (Ampicillin + Tetracycline) — Selection markers
  • Insertional inactivation: Insert DNA into resistance gene → that resistance lost → white colony
  • Blue-white selection: Recombinant = white colonies, Non-recombinant = blue
🖨️ PCR (Polymerase Chain Reaction)
  • Kary Mullis ने develop किया — Nobel Prize 1993
  • DNA amplification — छोटी मात्रा से millions of copies
  • 3 steps: Denaturation (94°C) → Annealing (40-60°C, primers) → Extension (72°C, Taq polymerase)
  • Thermostable Taq polymerase (Thermus aquaticus bacteria से)
  • Applications: Forensic, Genetic testing, COVID detection
🔬 Gel Electrophoresis
  • DNA fragments size के according separate करना
  • Agarose gel — Ethidium bromide stain (UV light में orange bands)
  • Small fragments → farther migrate | Large → closer to well
🧫 Transformation & Cloning
  • Competent cells — बने cells जो foreign DNA ले सकती हैं (CaCl2 treatment)
  • Bioreactor: Large scale production — Stirred tank, Sparged
  • Downstream processing: Separation → Purification → Formulation
⭐ PNST Important Points
  • EcoRI = First restriction enzyme (Palindromic sequence)
  • PCR = Kary Mullis (Nobel 1993), Taq polymerase
  • pBR322 = First artificial recombinant plasmid
  • Blue-white selection: Recombinant=white, Non-recombinant=blue
  • DNA ligase = Molecular glue

जीव और समष्टियाँ
Adaptations | Population | Interactions | Ecology
▼
Ecology = Greek "Oikos" (house) + "logos" (study)। Ernst Haeckel ने term introduce किया। Population = Same species के individuals का group।
🌡️ अनुकूलन | Adaptations
  • Temperature: Thermoregulators (endotherms: birds, mammals) vs Conformers (ectotherms: fish, reptiles)
  • Desert adaptations: Kangaroo rat — metabolic water, concentrated urine, nocturnal
  • Aquatic: Osmoregulation — Marine fish (lose water, drink salt water)
  • Altitude sickness: Low O2 → RBC production बढ़ता है (acclimatization)
  • Allen's rule: Cold climates में animals के appendages छोटे होते हैं
📊 Population विशेषताएं | Population Characteristics
  • Birth rate (Natality): Per capita births | Death rate (Mortality): Per capita deaths
  • Age structure: Pre-reproductive, Reproductive, Post-reproductive
  • Sex ratio: Males : Females
  • Exponential growth: dN/dt = rN (unlimited resources) — J-shaped curve
  • Logistic growth: dN/dt = rN(K-N)/K — S-shaped curve। K = Carrying capacity
  • r-strategists: Small, short life, many offspring | K-strategists: Large, long life, few offspring
🤝 जनसंख्या अंतःक्रिया | Population Interactions
  • Mutualism (+/+): Both benefit — Lichens (Algae+Fungi), Mycorrhiza, Clownfish+Anemone
  • Competition (-/-): Both harmed — Gause's competitive exclusion principle
  • Predation (+/-): Predator benefits, Prey harmed — Controls prey population
  • Parasitism (+/-): Parasite benefits, Host harmed — Cuscuta (Dodder plant)
  • Commensalism (+/0): One benefits, other unaffected — Barnacles on whale, Orchid on tree
  • Amensalism (-/0): One harmed, other unaffected — Penicillium vs bacteria
⭐ PNST Important Points
  • Logistic growth → S-curve → K = Carrying capacity
  • Mutualism = Lichens (Algae + Fungi symbiosis)
  • Gause's principle: Two species same niche में नहीं रह सकते
  • Kangaroo rat — desert adaptation (metabolic water)
  • Commensalism = Barnacle on whale

Productivity | Decomposition | Energy Flow | Nutrient Cycling
▼
Ecosystem = Biotic (living) + Abiotic (non-living) components की functional unit। A.G. Tansley ने 1935 में term introduce किया।
🌿 उत्पादकता | Productivity
  • GPP (Gross Primary Productivity): Total photosynthesis rate
  • NPP (Net Primary Productivity): GPP - Respiration। Available for consumers
  • Secondary Productivity: Consumers द्वारा new organic matter बनाना
  • Tropical forests = Maximum productivity | Desert = Minimum
🍂 अपघटन | Decomposition
  • Detrivores + Decomposers (Bacteria, Fungi) → Organic matter → Inorganic
  • Steps: Fragmentation → Leaching → Catabolism → Humification → Mineralisation
  • Humus: Dark colored, colloidal organic matter। Nutrient reservoir
  • Warm+Moist = Fast decomposition | Cold+Dry = Slow
⚡ ऊर्जा प्रवाह | Energy Flow
  • 10% rule (Lindeman, 1942): One trophic level से next → only 10% energy transfer
  • Producers → Primary consumers → Secondary → Tertiary
  • Food chain: Grass→Deer→Tiger | Grass→Insects→Frog→Snake→Eagle
  • Detritus food chain: Dead matter → Decomposers (dominant in forest)
  • Energy flow is unidirectional!
📊 पारिस्थितिक पिरामिड | Ecological Pyramids
  • Pyramid of numbers: Inverted in tree ecosystem (1 tree → many insects)
  • Pyramid of biomass: Inverted in aquatic (phytoplankton < zooplankton)
  • Pyramid of energy: Always upright (10% rule)
🔄 पोषण चक्र | Nutrient Cycling
  • Carbon cycle: Photosynthesis ↔ Respiration + Decomposition + Combustion
  • Phosphorus cycle: Sedimentary cycle (no atmospheric phase)
  • Atmosphere → N2 fixation → Nitrification → Denitrification
  • Ecosystem services: Pollination, O2, Water purification, Soil formation (Estimated $33 trillion/year)
⭐ PNST Important Points
  • 10% rule = Lindeman (1942)
  • Pyramid of energy = Always upright
  • NPP = GPP - Respiration
  • A.G. Tansley = Coined "Ecosystem" (1935)
  • Phosphorus cycle = Sedimentary (no gaseous phase)

Hotspots | Threats | In-situ | Ex-situ | IUCN
▼
Biodiversity = Variety of life on Earth। Robert May — Earth पर ~7 million species। Currently known = 1.5 million species। Edward Wilson ने "Biodiversity" term दिया।
📊 जैव विविधता के प्रकार | Types of Biodiversity
  • Genetic diversity: Same species में genetic variation। e.g., Rice — 50,000 varieties; Mango — 1000+ varieties in India
  • Species diversity: Number of species in an area
  • Ecosystem diversity: Different ecosystems (Desert, Rainforest, Mangrove)
  • Tropics में species diversity अधिक होती है (Species area relationship)
  • Alexander von Humboldt: "Species richness increases towards tropics"
📈 Species Area Relationship
  • log S = log C + Z log A (S=species, A=area, Z=slope, C=y-intercept)
  • Z value: 0.1-0.2 (small areas) → 0.6-1.2 (large areas, continents)
🗺️ Biodiversity Hotspots
  • Norman Myers ने concept दिया
  • Globally 34 hotspots (previously 25)
  • 2 hotspots in India: Western Ghats + Sri Lanka और Himalaya (Eastern)
  • Criteria: Endemic species >1500 plants + 70% habitat lost
⚠️ जैव विविधता को खतरे | Threats (HIPPO)
  • Habitat loss (most important!) — Deforestation, urbanization
  • Invasive species — Lantana, Parthenium (Carrot grass), Water hyacinth (Eichhornia)
  • Pollution
  • Population growth + Over exploitation
  • Over exploitation — Hunting, Poaching
  • 6th Mass Extinction: Present extinction rate 100-1000 times faster
🛡️ संरक्षण | Conservation
  • In-situ conservation (On site): Protected areas — Wildlife sanctuary, National park, Biosphere reserve, Sacred groves
  • India: 14 Biosphere reserves, 90 National parks, 500+ Wildlife sanctuaries
  • Ex-situ conservation (Off site): Zoological parks, Botanical gardens, Wildlife safari, Seed banks, Gene banks, Tissue culture
  • Cryopreservation = -196°C में gametes/embryo store
  • IUCN Red List: Extinct, Critically Endangered, Endangered, Vulnerable, Near Threatened
  • CBD (Convention on Biological Diversity): 1992, Rio de Janeiro ("Earth Summit")
  • World Biodiversity Day: May 22
⭐ PNST Important Points
  • Hotspots = 34 globally, 2 in India (Western Ghats, E. Himalaya)
  • Habitat loss = Most important threat to biodiversity
  • In-situ = National Park | Ex-situ = Zoo, Seed bank
  • CBD = 1992, Rio de Janeiro (Earth Summit)
  • Water hyacinth = Eichhornia crassipes (invasive, "Terror of Bengal")
  • Sacred groves = Community protected areas
